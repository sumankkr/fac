About Us

Welcome to FitBeau.com. Our influencer marketing platform is where brands can match with content creators to promote their fitness and beauty products

FitBeau was spawned from a fitness and beauty directory site built in 2008 which used facebook and twitter to allow businesses to use their own customers to do their ‘advertising’ for them. At the time Facebook was 2 years old (publicly) and twitter only had Steven Fry as their celeb advocate but this was the very start of the future of social media for businesses. With our team of fitness and beauty experts, analysts, and developers, and our deep understanding of the evolution of social media, we want to share our insights for you to capitalise on the newest trends fast, so that you stay ahead of the curve.
